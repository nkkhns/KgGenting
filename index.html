    Kampung Genting Community

    Hello,

    Kampung Genting is a village located in Balik Pulau, in Penang, which is a state in Malaysia. To the east is Kampung Genting Dalam while to the northeast is Kampung Terang. To the southeast is Kampung Perlis. Small village Kampung Genting is located in Balik Pulau. It is the first town that pass when we travel down the Balik Pulau hill road coming from the direction of Teluk Kumbar. It is situated at the foot of Bukit Genting. The intersection of Jalan Balik Pulau, Jalan Pondok Upeh, Jalan Pulau Betong, and Jalan Sungai Nipah forms Kampung Genting. The pecan, or village centre, is also known as Pekan Simpang Empat, which means the village centre of four intersections, as a result. One of Penang Islandâ€™s few remaining paddy fields is located to the west of Kampung Genting. Furthermore, the name "Genting" means "hill pass," and it refers to the constrained passageway that rural villages used to travel to Teluk Kumbar, Sungai Batu, and Bayan Lepas. Chinese settlers live in Kampung Genting's pekan, or village centre, which includes a market. Malay village homes are located further away from the pekan.
